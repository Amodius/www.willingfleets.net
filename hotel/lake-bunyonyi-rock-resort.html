Description

Lake Bunyonyi Rock Resort is your number one affordable and reliable lodging facility located  on the shores of Lake Bunyonyi.

Established in 2013, the resort specializes   in offering luxury food and sleeping services at affordable rates with un parallel value.

The resort offers a wide range of accommodations that are suitable for all types of travelers; from spacious luxury cottages with stunning views of the islands to affordable non self contained rooms. 

Lake Bunyonyi Rock Resort  is one of the Paradise on earth a must stay on your Uganda trip to south western region.

Hotel Policies
CHILDREN’S POLICY
Lake Bunyonyi Rock Resort also welcomes children of all ages. All children below 5 years stay free of charge with their parents Children below 12 years pay $30 pp per night for an extra bed (exclusive of meals) Children aged 12 to 15 years pay $50 pp for the bed All children aged from 5 to 11 years pay $20 per night when using an exiting bed Children from 12 to 15 years pay$30 pp when using an extra bed.
