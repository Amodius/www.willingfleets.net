Description

Gahiza Island Retreat is exclusively nested in the middle of Lake Mutanda. Our spacious eco cabins have a balcony, with the trademark sunrise.

 

Each of the cabin is self contained with hot water!
We offer accommodation in single, double/twin Tripple and family set ups, all under expansive mosquito nets.
